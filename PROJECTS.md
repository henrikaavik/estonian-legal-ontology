## Gas Trade Service (B2B LPG Marketplace)
- **Website:** https://gastradeservice.com/
- **Business:** B2B marketplace for LPG (liquefied petroleum gas) trading
- **Model:** Reverse auction - buyers post requests, sellers bid anonymously
- **Key feature:** Buyers see only winning bid, must purchase or lose 10% deposit
- **Commission:** 5% platform fee
- **Additional revenue:** Gas equipment sales and rental
- **Smart flow:** IoT sensors on tanks monitor levels, automatically trigger new auctions when low
